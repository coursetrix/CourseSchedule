About Coursetrix

As an Instructional Designer and Educational Technologist, I got tired of wrestling with Word tables to plan course designs. I built Coursetrix to solve that problem: a simple tool that lets educators blueprint their course structure (modules, assignments, and learning outcomes) before diving into LMS development.

This is an open source project available on GitHub.
