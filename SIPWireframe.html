Syllabus Information Page (SIP)

Your roadmap for college policies, student rights, and success resources. While your instructor handles course specifics, the SIP provides the universal rules and support available to all Cochise students.

Understanding College Jargon

Office Hours

These are not times when your instructor is hiding from you. They are guaranteed meeting times for you to ask questions or get mentorship.

Census Date

The official "snapshot" of who is in class. Missing your census activity can lead to an automatic drop and financial aid complications.

Incomplete Grade

A temporary "pause button" if a documented emergency hits after you've already completed 80% of the course work.

Dropping a Class

Thinking about leaving? Talk to your instructor first. There may be other options available, or they can at least help you complete the proper paperwork.
