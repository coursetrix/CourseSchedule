Welcome to Coursetrix

Coursetrix helps you design your course structure before building it in your LMS. Here's how it works:

1
Set Up Your Course

Enter your course name and date range. This gives Coursetrix the boundaries it needs to build your schedule.

2
Define Learning Outcomes

Add your Course-Level Learning Outcomes (CLLOs) and optionally your Program-Level Learning Outcomes (PLLOs) via the Manage PLLOs button. Align CLLOs to PLLOs to enable the coverage report.

3
Set Assignment Types

Customize the categories used for your assignments — such as Discussion, Quiz, or Project. A point summary by type is generated automatically as you build your schedule.

4
Build Your Schedule

Create modules and fill them with assignments. Set due dates, point values, and CLLO links. Drag and drop to reorder anything.

5
Export Your Plan

Generate a PDF report, download a CSV, or copy the table directly into Word or your LMS.

Planning a new semester?

Import a saved course, update your start and end dates, then hit Redistribute Dates to automatically re-space all your modules and assignments across the new date range.

What's New in Coursetrix

v3.0 — Program-Level Learning Outcomes
  • Program-Level Learning Outcomes (PLLOs) — Define program outcomes via the Manage PLLOs button and align CLLOs to them directly in Course Setup.
  • PLLO Coverage Report — A dedicated report showing which program outcomes your course addresses, with supporting assignments grouped by unit. PLLOs not covered by the course are flagged clearly.
  • Collapsible report panels — Course Schedule Report and PLLO Coverage Report panels collapse to keep the workspace clean.
Core Features
  • Course Setup — Course name, date range, and assignment types with a live point summary.
  • Learning Outcomes — CLLOs and PLLOs with full two-level alignment mapping.
  • Schedule Builder — Modules and assignments with drag-and-drop reordering across modules.
  • Redistribute Dates — Re-space all modules and assignments across a new date range in one click.
  • Course Schedule Report — Export to PDF, copy to Word, Markdown, or CSV.
  • PLLO Coverage Report — Export program-level alignment to Word, Markdown, or CSV.
  • Auto-save — Everything saves automatically to your browser. Use Save Course to download a backup.
